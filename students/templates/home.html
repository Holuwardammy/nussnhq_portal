About NUSS

The National Union of Saki Students National Headquarter (NUSSNHQ) is dedicated to supporting Saki students through education, leadership, and community development. We aim to empower the next generation of leaders.

Our Mission

To empower Somali students through education, mentorship, and leadership opportunities.

Our Vision

To build a strong community of Somali students who contribute positively to society.

Our Community

We connect students across universities and create opportunities for collaboration and growth.

Our Programs

NUSS offers several programs designed to support Saki students academically, professionally, and socially.

Leadership Training

Developing future Somali leaders through workshops, seminars, and leadership opportunities.

Student Mentorship

Connecting experienced students with new members to guide them through university life.

Academic Support

Providing resources, tutoring, and study groups to help students succeed academically.

Networking Events

Creating opportunities for students to connect with professionals and industry leaders.
